March 2023

Machined Screw Plate and Screw Driver

Screw Plate: Followed detailed design schematic to fabricate a screw plate used to visualize and classify screws and screw holes. Using a CNC Milling Machine and Drill Press to measure, engrave, drill, and chamfer holes. Hand tapped the holes to make threads. And Hand polished.

Screw Driver: Forged the head of the screw driver out of a steel rod and filed it down to shape. Head treated and annealed the tip for desired strenght. For the handle, I milled flutes into a stock piece of delrin and turned it on the lathe for an ergonomic shape. Lastly, I knurled the end of the rod and press fit it into the handle for the final product.
